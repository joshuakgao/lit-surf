# Synthetic Datasets — Index

Synthetic datasets provide an economical and scalable alternative to real-world annotation by generating computer-rendered images with automatically-produced dense per-pixel labels. This wiki tracks literature on high-quality synthetic dataset design, photorealistic rendering, comprehensive ground truth generation, and sim-to-real transfer performance across scene understanding tasks.
