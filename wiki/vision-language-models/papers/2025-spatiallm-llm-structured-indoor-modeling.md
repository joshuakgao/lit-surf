## Brief

SpatialLM extends LLMs to process 3D point clouds and generate structured scene descriptions (architectural layout + object bounding boxes) for indoor environments. Despite being filed here initially, this paper is primarily a 3D scene understanding contribution rather than a vision-language modeling advance.
