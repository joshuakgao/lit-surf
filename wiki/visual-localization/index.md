# Visual Localization — Index

Visual localization is the task of determining the 6-DoF pose of a camera within a known 3D scene. This wiki tracks literature on robust and efficient localization methods, with emphasis on learned features, hierarchical matching, and real-time operation on resource-constrained devices. Applications include autonomous robotics, augmented reality, and structure-from-motion.
