# Dynamic Scene Graph Generation — Index

Scene graph generation is the task of constructing structured, graph-based representations of images and videos where objects are nodes and relationships are edges. Dynamic scene graph generation extends this to video, requiring temporal reasoning about how relationships and interactions evolve across frames. This wiki tracks methods, datasets, and applications in this emerging area.
